Welcome to Riverside Community Centre

We are a vibrant community hub serving residents of all ages and abilities. Our mission is to provide inclusive programs, activities, and services that strengthen our community and improve quality of life for everyone.

From fitness classes to arts programs, youth activities to senior services, we offer something for every member of our diverse community.

Explore Our Programs

What We Offer

Fitness & Wellness

Modern gym facilities, yoga classes, swimming pool, and wellness programs designed for all fitness levels and abilities.

Arts & Culture

Creative workshops, art studios, music lessons, and cultural events that celebrate our diverse community heritage.

Youth Programs

After-school care, sports leagues, summer camps, and educational activities for children and teenagers.

Senior Services

Social clubs, health programs, technology training, and support services for older adults in our community.

Community Events

Regular festivals, markets, workshops, and gatherings that bring our neighborhood together throughout the year.

Support Services

Counseling, job training, language classes, and assistance programs for those who need extra support.

Join Our Community

Membership is open to all residents. Enjoy access to our facilities, discounted program fees, and be part of a welcoming, inclusive community.
