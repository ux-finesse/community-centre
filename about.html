About Riverside Community Centre

For over 35 years, Riverside Community Centre has been the heart of our neighborhood, providing a welcoming space where people of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities come together to learn, grow, and thrive.

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity and ensures everyone has access to the programs, services, and support they need to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To strengthen our community by providing accessible, inclusive programs and services that enhance quality of life, promote health and wellbeing, and foster connections among all residents.

Vision Statement

A vibrant, connected community where every person has the opportunity to participate, contribute, and belong, regardless of age, ability, background, or circumstance.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone and actively work to remove barriers that prevent participation. Our programs and facilities are designed to be accessible to people of all abilities.

Community

We believe in the power of connection. By bringing people together, we create a stronger, more supportive neighborhood where everyone looks out for one another.

Respect

We treat every individual with dignity and respect, valuing the unique perspectives, experiences, and contributions each person brings to our community.

Excellence

We strive for quality in everything we do, from our programs and facilities to our customer service, ensuring our community receives the best possible experience.

Innovation

We continuously adapt and evolve to meet changing community needs, embracing new ideas and approaches to better serve our diverse population.

Collaboration

We partner with local organizations, schools, and businesses to maximize resources and create comprehensive support networks for our community.

Our History

Riverside Community Centre opened its doors in 1989 as a small neighborhood gathering space. What began as a single room with basic facilities has grown into a comprehensive community hub serving over 15,000 residents annually.

Key milestones in our journey include:

  • 1989: Centre opens with fitness classes and youth programs
  • 1995: Major expansion adds swimming pool and gymnasium
  • 2002: Launch of senior services and support programs
  • 2008: Arts wing opened with studios and performance space
  • 2015: Complete accessibility renovation ensuring barrier-free access
  • 2020: Digital transformation with online programs during pandemic
  • 2023: New community garden and outdoor recreation area completed

Our Accessibility Commitment

Accessibility is at the core of everything we do. We are committed to ensuring that all community members, regardless of physical, sensory, cognitive, or other abilities, can fully participate in our programs and access our facilities.

Our accessibility features include:

  • Wheelchair accessible entrances, elevators, and facilities throughout the building
  • Accessible parking spaces close to all entrances
  • Hearing loop systems in all meeting rooms and program spaces
  • Large print and audio versions of all program materials available upon request
  • Trained staff to assist with accessibility needs
  • Adaptive equipment available for fitness programs
  • Sensory-friendly spaces and programs for individuals with autism and sensory processing needs
  • Service animal friendly environment

We continuously review and improve our accessibility practices. If you have suggestions or encounter any barriers, please contact our Accessibility Coordinator.
